带有基本栅格的HTML代码

<div class="grid_container grid_c18_m10">
	<div class="grid_row">
		<div class="grid_5">
		</div>
		<div class="grid_13">
		</div>
	</div>
</div>

这样便创建了一个侧边栏为5格宽度（265px），主体内容为13格宽度（705px）的两栏栅格布局

grid_5 265px
grid_13 705px

微调基本栅格

栅格系统的最小调整单位为45px，如果预设的宽度满足不了需求，可使用pull与push的class进行微调

比如：默认为gird_7与grid_11

<div class="grid_container grid_c18_m10">
	<div class="grid_row">
		<div class="grid_7">
		</div>
		<div class="grid_11">
		</div>
	</div>
</div>
grid_7 375px
grid_11 595px

整体向左偏移5px

<div class="grid_container grid_c18_m10">
	<div class="grid_row">
		<div class="grid_7_pull5">
		</div>
		<div class="grid_11_push5">
		</div>
	</div>
</div>
grid_7_pull5 370px(375px-5px)
grid_11_push5 600px(595px+5px)

整体向右偏移5px

<div class="grid_container grid_c18_m10">
	<div class="grid_row">
		<div class="grid_7_push5">
		</div>
		<div class="grid_11_pull5">
		</div>
	</div>
</div>
grid_7_push5 380px(375px+5px)
grid_11_pull5 590px(595px-5px)
